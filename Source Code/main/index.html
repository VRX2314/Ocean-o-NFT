Question

Learn answers to frequently asked questions on Ocean O'NFT

Is there a minimum bid requirement?

Bids must be at least 5% higher than the previous bid. Only bids in the same payment token (e.g. wrapped ETH or DAI) as the auction will be counted towards the winning bid.

What is the longest I can list an item for?

Currently, the longest you can set a fixed price listing is for 6 months.

What file formats can I use to make NFTs?

You can use a variety of file formats for images, videos, audio or even 3D models to make your NFT (see below). The maximum file size supported is 100MB, but we recommend keeping it under 100MB for faster load times.

How do I buy fixed-price NFTs?

Buying items with a fixed price on Ocean O'NFT. Select the Buy Now button and follow the prompts in your wallet. Once the transaction is complete, the item will transfer to your wallet and the seller will receive the funds.

What is Freezing Metadata?

Freezing your metadata will permanently lock and store it on decentralized file storage, which allows your data to be accessible for other clients to view and use. This means, your item will forever be safely stored and will never be lost or missing.

Does Ocean O'NFT allow physical item trades?

Ocean O'NFT is happy to facilitate NFT sales that involve a legal transfer of physical goods or rights, but it is the seller’s responsibility to both:
1) ensure the legality of that transfer,
2) coordinate with the buyer (through the item unlockable content section) in order to ensure those items are delivered as expected.
